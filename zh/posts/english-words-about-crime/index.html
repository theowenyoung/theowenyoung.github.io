犯罪相关的英文单词汇总

这是我在 Anki 上标记为 crime Tag 的一些单词，比如 jail(监狱), arrest(逮捕)，记录如下：

Steal-偷窃

ItemValue
单词steal
中译偷窃
音标[stiːl]
MeaningTo steal is to take something that is not yours.
ExampleThe men tried to steal money from the bank.

Thief-小偷

ItemValue
单词thief
中译小偷
音标[θiːf]
MeaningA thief is someone who quietly takes things that do not belong to them.
ExampleA thief broke into our home and took my mother’s jewelry.

Jail-监狱

ItemValue
单词jail
中译监狱
音标[dʒeil]
MeaningA jail is a place to keep bad people.
ExampleHe was sent to jail for taking other people’s cars.

Victim-受害者

ItemValue
单词victim
中译受害者
音标[víktim]
MeaningA victim is a person who is hurt by a bad action.
ExampleI was a victim of a robbery.

Trial-审判

ItemValue
单词trial
中译审判
音标[tráiəl]
MeaningA trial is the way a court discovers if a person is guilty or innocent.
ExampleHe went on trial for robbing the bank.

Arrest-逮捕

ItemValue
单词arrest
中译逮捕
音标[ərést]
MeaningTo arrest someone means to catch that person for doing something bad.
ExampleThe man was arrested for breaking the law.

Convict-被宣判有罪

ItemValue
单词convict
中译有罪
音标[kənvíkt]
MeaningTo convict means to prove that someone did a bad thing.
ExampleHe was convicted of the crime and sent to jail.

Innocent-无辜的

ItemValue
单词innocent
中译无辜的
音标[ínəsnt]
MeaningAn innocent person is not guilty of a crime.
ExampleThe judge said that the woman was innocent of the crime.

Kidnap-绑架

ItemValue
单词kidnap
中译绑架
音标[kídnæ̀p]
MeaningTo kidnap someone is to take that person illegally.
ExampleShe was terrified to find out her son had been kidnapped.

Confess-招供

ItemValue
单词confess
中译招供
音标[kənfés]
MeaningTo confess is to admit a bad or embarrassing truth.
ExampleThe woman confessed that she had stolen the money.

Outlaw-不法之徒

ItemValue
单词outlaw
中译不法之徒
音标[áutlɔ̀ː]
MeaningAn outlaw is a criminal who hides from the police.
ExampleThe police passed out posters of the outlaw to all the people.

Sin-罪过

ItemValue
单词sin
中译罪过
音标[sin]
MeaningA sin is something that is wrong for religious reasons.
ExampleTaking something that doesn’t belong to you is a sin.

Detain-扣留

ItemValue
单词detain
中译扣留
音标[ditéin]
MeaningTo detain is to officially prevent someone from leaving a place.
ExampleThe police had to detain the thief for shoplifting.

Prisoner-囚犯

ItemValue
单词prisoner
中译囚犯
音标[prízənər]
MeaningA prisoner is someone who is taken by force and kept somewhere.
ExampleHe has been a prisoner for many years.

Violate-违反规定

ItemValue
单词violate
中译违反规定
音标[váiəlèit]
MeaningTo violate a law, rule, or agreement means to break it.
ExampleI was given a ticket because the policeman said I violated a traffic law.

Fraud-欺诈

ItemValue
单词fraud
中译欺诈
音标[frɔːd]
MeaningFraud is the crime of gaining money by lying or by tricking people.
ExampleExperts say that credit card fraud increases around the holidays.

Villain-“恶棍”

ItemValue
单词villain
中译“恶棍”
音标[vílən]
MeaningA villain is an evil person who breaks the law or hurts others.
ExampleThe villain made of with half of my family’s valuables.

Assassin-刺客

ItemValue
单词assassin
中译刺客
音标[əsǽsn]
MeaningAn assassin is someone who murders an important person.
ExampleAn assassin killed Martin Luther King on April 4, 1968.

Conspiracy-共谋

ItemValue
单词conspiracy
中译共谋
音标[kənspírəsi]
MeaningA conspiracy is a secret plan that two or more people make together to do something harmful or illegal.
ExampleThe businessman thought there was a conspiracy among his coworkers to get him fired.

Custody-羁押

ItemValue
单词custody
中译羁押
音标[kΛstədi]
MeaningCustody is the right to take care of someone or something.
ExampleThe prisoners were put in the custody of the jailers.

Crime-犯罪行为

ItemValue
单词crime
中译犯罪行为
音标[kraim]
MeaningIf someone commits a crime, he or she does something bad that can be punished by law.
ExamplePolice quickly arrived at the scene of the crime.

Offense-犯罪行为

ItemValue
单词offense
中译犯罪行为
音标[əféns]
MeaningAn offense is an action that breaks the law and requires punishment.
ExampleHe was put in jail for two days for the offense.

Implicate-牵涉其中

ItemValue
单词implicate
中译牵涉其中
音标[ímplikèit]
MeaningTo implicate someone is to show that they have done a crime or something bad.
ExampleThe man was implicated in the theft at the store.

Amnesty-大赦国际

ItemValue
单词amnesty
中译大赦国际
音标[ǽmnəsti:]
MeaningAmnesty is a pardon given to prisoners of war.
ExampleShe was denied amnesty for her involvement in the war.

Captive-俘虏

ItemValue
单词captive
中译俘虏
音标[kǽptiv]
MeaningA captive is a prisoner.
ExampleThe guards told the captive that there was no way he could escape the prison.

Culprit-罪魁祸首

ItemValue
单词culprit
中译罪魁祸首
音标[kʌ́lprit]
MeaningA culprit is someone who has committed a crime or other bad deed.
ExampleThe police were still searching for the culprit from the robbery.

Ransom-赎金

ItemValue
单词ransom
中译赎金
音标[rǽnsəm]
MeaningA ransom is a sum of money paid to a kidnapper to set the person free.
ExampleHe kidnapped the prince and demanded $1 million as a ransom.
