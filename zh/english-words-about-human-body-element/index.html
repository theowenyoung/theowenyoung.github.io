人体元素相关的英文单词

这是我在 Anki 上标记为人体所需元素 Tag 的一些单词，如蛋白质，淀粉之类的，记录如下：

Carbohydrate-碳水化合物

ItemValue
单词carbohydrate
中译碳水化合物
音标[kὰːrbouháidreit]
MeaningCarbohydrates are substances in foods like bread that give energy.
ExampleCarbohydrates like rice are a good source of energy for active people.

Glucose-葡萄糖

ItemValue
单词glucose
中译葡萄糖
音标[glúːkous]
MeaningGlucose is a natural form of sugar that exists in plants and fruit.
ExamplePeople with diabetes need to check their glucose levels.

Starch-淀粉

ItemValue
单词starch
中译淀粉
音标[staːrʧ]
MeaningStarch is a substance that provides your body with energy and is found in foods such as wheat, potatoes, and rice.
ExamplePasta is high in starch.

Protein-蛋白

ItemValue
单词protein
中译蛋白
音标[próutiːn]
MeaningProtein is a substance that is necessary for the body to grow and be strong.
ExampleFish and beef are good sources of protein.

Organic-有机食品

ItemValue
单词organic
中译有机食品
音标[ɔːrgǽnik]
MeaningIf food is organic, it is grown without adding chemicals to it.
ExampleThe organic carrots are more expensive, but they’re better for you.
